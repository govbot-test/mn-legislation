(a) In cases where the petitioner contests the valuation of income-producing property, the following information must be provided to the county assessor no later than August 1 of the taxes payable year: (1) a year-end financial statement for the year prior to the assessment date; (2) a year-end financial statement for the year of the assessment date; (3) a rent roll on or near the assessment date listing the tenant name, lease start and end dates, base rent, square footage leased and vacant space; (4) identification of all lease agreements not disclosed on a rent roll in the response to clause (3), listing the tenant name, lease start and end dates, base rent, and square footage leased; (5) net rentable square footage of the building or buildings; and (6) anticipated income and expenses in the form of a proposed budget for the year subsequent to the year of the assessment date. The petitioner shall furnish to the county assessor at least five days before the hearing under this chapter with the property's appraisal, if any, which will be presented to the court at the hearing.
