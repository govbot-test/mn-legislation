Version List HF4251 Authors and Status PDF Rtf Bill Text Versions Engrossments Introduction PDF Posted on 03/12/2026 Jump to page/line # Line numbers 1.1 1.2 1.3 1.4 1.5 1.6 1.7 1.8 1.9 1.10 1.11 1.12 1.13 1.14 1.15 1.16 1.17 A bill for an act relating to taxation; local government aids; establishing Capitol complex county host program aid; appropriating money; proposing coding for new law in Minnesota Statutes, chapter 477A. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin [477A.41] CAPITOL COMPLEX COUNTY HOST PROGRAM. new text end new text begin Beginning with aids payable in 2027, the commissioner of revenue shall pay to Ramsey County $5 per square foot of state-owned buildings located in the Capitol Area described in section 15B.02, paragraph (b). The purpose of the aid is to compensate Ramsey County for the loss of tax base and disproportionate impact of state ownership of buildings located in the Capitol Area. Aid payments under this section must be used to reduce current year property taxes levied on net tax capacity within the county. An amount sufficient to pay the aid under this section is annually appropriated from the general fund to the commissioner of revenue. The commissioner shall pay the aid annually at the times provided in section 477A.015. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective for aids payable in calendar year 2027 and thereafter. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
