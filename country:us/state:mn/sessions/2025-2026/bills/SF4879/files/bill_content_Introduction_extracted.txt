Sec. 4. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 142B.06, subdivision 5, is amended to read: Subd. 5. Child foster care license application. (a) The relatives with whom the emergency placement has been made shall complete the child foster care license application and necessary paperwork within ten days of the placement. Sec. 5. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 142B.40, subdivision 3, is amended to read: Subd. 3. License holder qualifications for child foster care.
