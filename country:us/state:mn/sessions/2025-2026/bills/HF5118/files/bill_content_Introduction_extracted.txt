BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin [477A.0178] AID REDUCTIONS FOR TAX BASE REDUCTIONS. new text end new text begin Subdivision 1. new text end new text begin Penalty for failure to approve tax base expansions. new text end new text begin Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, a county or a city that denies approval for a local development project that would have increased the jurisdiction's property tax base must have its aid under sections 477A.011 to 477A.03 reduced by the amount determined under subdivision 2. The commissioner of revenue must reduce county program aid paid to a county or local government aid paid to a city by the amount determined under subdivision 2 in the aids payable year following certification of the amount under subdivision 3. Reductions under this section continue until certification under subdivision 4. No aid payment may be less than zero as a result of reductions under this section, but the amount of the reduction that cannot be made out of a payment shall be applied to future payments until the total amount has been deducted. new text end new text begin Subd. 2. new text end new text begin Reduction amount. new text end new text begin Aid reductions under this section are equal to the amount certified under subdivision 3, paragraph (a), clause (2), multiplied by the amount certified under subdivision 3, paragraph (a), clause (3). new text end new text begin Subd. 3. new text end new text begin Certification of reduction. new text end new text begin (a) On July 1 of each year, the governing body of any county or city that, within the past year, has denied approval for a local development that would have increased the property tax base within the jurisdiction must notify the commissioner of revenue of the following: new text end new text begin (1) the jurisdiction's taxable net tax capacity in the current taxes payable year; new text end new text begin (2) the jurisdiction's net tax capacity tax rate for the current taxes payable year; and new text end new text begin (3) the amount of growth in net tax capacity the jurisdiction would have experienced had the local development not been denied. new text end new text begin (b) The commissioner of revenue must certify the aid reductions under this section for any jurisdiction that provides notification under paragraph (a). new text end new text begin Subd. 4. new text end new text begin Reinstatement of aid. new text end new text begin The governing body of a county or city subject to aid reductions under this section must notify the commissioner of revenue once the jurisdiction's taxable net tax capacity exceeds the sum of the amount under subdivision 3, paragraph (a), clause (1), plus the amount under subdivision 3, paragraph (a), clause (3). Upon receipt of the notification, the commissioner of revenue must verify the information and certify to the jurisdiction that they are no longer subject to aid reductions under this section. Beginning with aids payable in the year following notification under this subdivision, the commissioner of revenue must no longer make aid reductions under this section, unless the jurisdiction provides a new notice under subdivision 3, paragraph (a). new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective beginning June 30, 2027. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
