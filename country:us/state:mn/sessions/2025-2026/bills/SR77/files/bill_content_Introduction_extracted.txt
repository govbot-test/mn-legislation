No. 77, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on April 21, 2026 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2congratulating Kenley Kierstead-Hahn on her achievements in the sport of 1.3wrestling. 1.4WHEREAS, wrestling demands agility, strength, timing, and stamina in addition to reservoirs 1.5of psychological discipline and resolve; and 1.6WHEREAS, individual and team championships are highly sought honors for athletes of all 1.7ages; and 1.8WHEREAS, fifth-grader Kenley Kierstead-Hahn of Paynesville, Minnesota, has already 1.9built an impressive athletic resume; and 1.10WHEREAS, in 2023, Kenley won her first championship in girls folkstyle wrestling at the 1.11Northland Youth Wrestling State Tournament. To date, she has won four consecutive tournaments; 1.12and 1.13WHEREAS, Kenley made history in 2025 as the first wrestler from Paynesville to earn the 1.14USA Wrestling Triple Crown. This prestigious honor is awarded to athletes who win state titles in 1.15folkstyle, freestyle, and Greco-Roman wrestling; and 1.16WHEREAS, outside of wrestling, Kenley participates in cheerleading, softball, tackle football, 1.17gymnastics, and the Just for Kix dance program; and 1.18WHEREAS, known for her determination and strong work ethic, Kenley gives her best effort 1.19both on and off the mat. She is excited to continue learning, improving, and growing in all the 1.20sports she loves; and 1.21NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.22congratulates Kenley Kierstead-Hahn on her outstanding athletic accomplishments. 1.23BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.24enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.25Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Kenley Kierstead-Hahn.
