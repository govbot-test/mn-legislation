BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin [93.5141] ADVANCE TRIBAL REVIEW OF PROPOSED MINING PROJECTS. new text end new text begin Subdivision 1. new text end new text begin Definitions. new text end new text begin (a) For purposes of this section, the following terms have the meanings given. new text end new text begin (b) "Affected Tribe" means a Tribe whose reservation land is wholly or partially located within the same county as any portion of a proposed mining project or within an adjoining county. new text end new text begin (c) "Mining permit" means a permit to mine under section 93.481 or a permit for the production of gas or oil under section 93.513. new text end new text begin (d) "Mining project" means a project that requires a mining permit. new text end new text begin Subd. 2. new text end new text begin Timeline for permit review. new text end new text begin If there are one or more affected Tribes, the commissioner of natural resources may not begin reviewing an application for a mining permit until the earlier of: new text end new text begin (1) all affected Tribes: new text end new text begin (i) complete archeological and cultural review and all necessary preservation work and comply with all requirements of sections 138.31 to 138.42 and 307.08 in the event of inadvertent discoveries; and new text end new text begin (ii) submit a project review to the commissioner according to subdivision 3; or new text end new text begin (2) 24 months have elapsed since the initial application for the mining permit was submitted. new text end new text begin Subd. 3. new text end new text begin Review by affected Tribes required. new text end new text begin (a) If upon receiving an application for a mining permit the commissioner determines that there are one or more affected Tribes, the commissioner must inform each affected Tribe of that determination, share the permit application and related materials with each affected Tribe, and request that each affected Tribe submit a project review to the commissioner of natural resources for the proposed project as soon as possible. new text end new text begin (b) In developing a project review under this subdivision, an affected Tribe may examine and comment on all aspects of the proposed project and may make recommendations regarding whether the mining permit should be approved or denied, recommendations for permit conditions, and recommendations relating to any other aspects of the proposed project that the Tribe deems relevant. new text end new text begin Subd. 4. new text end new text begin Relation to other law. new text end new text begin The requirements of this section are in addition to the requirements of section 10.65. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
