For purposes of this section: (a) "Peace officer" or "officer" has the meaning given in section 626.84, subdivision 1, paragraph (c). (b) "Dependent" means a person who: (1) meets the definition of dependent in section 62L.02, subdivision 11, at the time of the officer'snew text begin , paramedic's, emergency medical technician's,new text end or firefighter's injury or death, or at the time of the volunteer firefighter's death; and (2) is not covered under another group health plan.
