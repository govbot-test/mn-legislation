BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 290.0132, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 36. new text end new text begin Expenses related to parking at a health facility. new text end new text begin (a) The amount of eligible health facility parking expenses in excess of $100 is a subtraction. new text end new text begin (b) For the purposes of this subdivision, the following terms have the meanings given: new text end new text begin (1) "eligible family member" means a spouse or qualifying relative, as defined in section 152(d) of the Internal Revenue Code; new text end new text begin (2) "eligible health facility" means a hospital or outpatient surgical center licensed under sections 144.50 to 144.56; physician office; medical or dental clinic; or birth center licensed under section 144.615; new text end new text begin (3) "eligible health facility parking expenses" means amounts actually paid in cash to park at an eligible health facility: new text end new text begin (i) by an eligible patient for the purpose of receiving health care at the eligible health facility; new text end new text begin (ii) by an individual for the purpose of visiting an eligible patient at the eligible health facility if the eligible patient is an eligible family member; or new text end new text begin (iii) by an employee of an eligible health facility for the purpose of working at the facility; and new text end new text begin (4) "eligible patient" means an individual receiving health care from an eligible health facility. new text end new text begin (c) The subtraction under this subdivision excludes amounts: new text end new text begin (1) deducted as medical expenses under section 290.0122, subdivision 6; new text end new text begin (2) paid or reimbursed from a health savings account, as defined in section 223(d) of the Internal Revenue Code; new text end new text begin (3) paid or reimbursed from a health flexible spending arrangement, as defined in section 106(c)(2) of the Internal Revenue Code; and new text end new text begin (4) for which the taxpayer was otherwise reimbursed, including but not limited to reimbursement by an employer. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective for taxable years beginning after December 31, 2024. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
