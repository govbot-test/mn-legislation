BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin [37.135] STATE AGRICULTURAL SOCIETY PUBLIC SAFETY ACCOUNT; APPROPRIATION; FEES. new text end new text begin Subdivision 1. new text end new text begin Establishment; appropriation. new text end new text begin A State Agricultural Society public safety account is established in the agricultural fund. Money in the account, including interest, is appropriated to the board of managers of the State Agricultural Society for grants to the cities of Falcon Heights and St. Paul to provide: new text end new text begin (1) public safety services, such as police, fire, and emergency medical responses; new text end new text begin (2) public works expenses for traffic management and sanitation; and new text end new text begin (3) neighborhood cleanup and restoration efforts. new text end new text begin Subd. 2. new text end new text begin Fees. new text end new text begin The board of managers of the State Agricultural Society shall charge a supplementary 30 cent fee on each admission ticket. All fees collected under this section shall be deposited in the state treasury and credited to the State Agricultural Society account in the agricultural fund under this section. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
