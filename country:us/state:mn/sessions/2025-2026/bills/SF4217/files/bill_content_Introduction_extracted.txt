Subd. 2. Special event services permit. (a) No person shall perform special event services without first obtaining a special event services permit from the board. (c) An individual providing services under a special event services permit may only perform services within the individual's specific field of licensure and as defined by the permit. The services provided pursuant to the special event services permit must comply with the requirements of this chapter and all federal, state, and local laws.
