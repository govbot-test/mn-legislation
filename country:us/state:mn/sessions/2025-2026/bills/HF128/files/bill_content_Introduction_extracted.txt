BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 609.324, subdivision 1, is amended to read: Subdivision 1. Engaging in, hiring, or agreeing to hire minor to engage in prostitution; penalties. (a) new text begin Whoever intentionally engages in prostitution with an individual under the age of 14 years may be sentenced to imprisonment for not more than 30 years or to payment of a fine of not more than $40,000, or both.new text end new text begin (b) new text end Whoever intentionally does any of the following may be sentenced to imprisonment for not more than 20 years or to payment of a fine of not more than $40,000, or both: deleted text begin (1) engages in prostitution with an individual under the age of 14 years; deleted text end deleted text begin (2)deleted text end new text begin (1)new text end hires or offers or agrees to hire an individual under the age of 14 years to engage in sexual penetration or sexual contact; or deleted text begin (3)deleted text end new text begin (2)new text end hires or offers or agrees to hire an individual who the actor reasonably believes to be under the age of 14 years to engage in sexual penetration or sexual contact. new text begin (c) Whoever intentionally engages in prostitution with an individual under the age of 16 years but at least 14 years may be sentenced to imprisonment for not more than 15 years or to payment of a fine of not more than $30,000, or both. new text end deleted text begin (b)deleted text end new text begin (d)new text end Whoever intentionally does any of the following may be sentenced to imprisonment for not more than ten years or to payment of a fine of not more than $20,000, or both: deleted text begin (1) engages in prostitution with an individual under the age of 16 years but at least 14 years; deleted text end deleted text begin (2)deleted text end new text begin (1)new text end hires or offers or agrees to hire an individual under the age of 16 years but at least 14 years to engage in sexual penetration or sexual contact; or deleted text begin (3)deleted text end new text begin (2)new text end hires or offers or agrees to hire an individual who the actor reasonably believes to be under the age of 16 years but at least 14 years to engage in sexual penetration or sexual contact. new text begin (e) Whoever intentionally engages in prostitution with an individual under the age of 18 years but at least 16 years may be sentenced to imprisonment for not more than ten years or to payment of a fine of not more than $20,000, or both. new text end deleted text begin (c)deleted text end new text begin (f)new text end Whoever intentionally does any of the following may be sentenced to imprisonment for not more than five years or to payment of a fine of not more than $10,000, or both: deleted text begin (1) engages in prostitution with an individual under the age of 18 years but at least 16 years; deleted text end deleted text begin (2)deleted text end new text begin (1)new text end hires or offers or agrees to hire an individual under the age of 18 years but at least 16 years to engage in sexual penetration or sexual contact; or deleted text begin (3)deleted text end new text begin (2)new text end hires or offers or agrees to hire an individual who the actor reasonably believes to be under the age of 18 years but at least 16 years to engage in sexual penetration or sexual contact. new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective August 1, 2025, and applies to crimes committed on or after that date. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
