BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin [161.375] COST PARTICIPATION. new text end new text begin Subdivision 1. new text end new text begin Cost participation limitations. new text end new text begin (a) Cost participation policies and procedures established by the commissioner for trunk highway projects must not require or obligate a local unit of government to pay for any portion of the project costs that are: new text end new text begin (1) incurred within a trunk highway right-of-way; and new text end new text begin (2) necessitated as a result of the project. new text end new text begin (b) Project costs under this subdivision include but are not limited to utility relocation. new text end new text begin (c) The requirements under this subdivision apply regardless of facility ownership, whether an expenditure is for a nontrunk highway purpose that must not be paid from the trunk highway fund, or whether the project provides for nontrunk highway system benefits. The commissioner must identify nontrunk highway fund sources as necessary. new text end new text begin Subd. 2. new text end new text begin Application. new text end new text begin The requirements under subdivision 1 do not apply to costs that are a result of agreement between the commissioner and the local unit of government for work that is in addition to the project design or scope, including as provided under section 161.38, subdivisions 1 and 2. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective July 1, 2025. This section does not apply to a trunk highway project that was included in the state transportation improvement program before July 1, 2025, for commencement of construction in calendar year 2025. new text end Sec. 2. new text begin APPROPRIATION; TRUNK HIGHWAY PROJECT COSTS.new text end new text begin $....... in fiscal year 2026 and $....... in fiscal year 2027 are appropriated from the general fund to the commissioner of transportation for the costs of trunk highway projects that are not eligible to be paid from the trunk highway fund, in conformance with the requirements under Minnesota Statutes, section 161.375, subdivision 1. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
