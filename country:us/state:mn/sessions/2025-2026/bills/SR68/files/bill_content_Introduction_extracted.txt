No. 68, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on March 25, 2026 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2recognizing Minnesota's Year of Water. 1.3WHEREAS, water shapes Minnesota's identity, economy, culture, and quality of life; and 1.4WHEREAS, Minnesota's lakes, rivers, wetlands, groundwater, and drinking water systems 1.5are shared resources that connect communities across geography and generations; and 1.6WHEREAS, Minnesotans from across the state, including rural and urban communities, 1.7landowners, educators, youth, civic organizations, and local leaders, have demonstrated broad 1.8interest in elevating water as a shared public value; and 1.9WHEREAS, stewardship of water is strengthened when citizens, communities, and public 1.10institutions work together voluntarily and locally; and 1.11WHEREAS, recognition of water as a shared inheritance offers an opportunity to unite 1.12Minnesotans across differences without creating mandates, policy directives, or fiscal obligations; 1.13NOW, THEREFORE, 1.14BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it recognizes June 1, 2026, 1.15through May 31, 2027, as Minnesota's Year of Water. 1.16BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that this recognition is intended to encourage voluntary civic 1.17awareness, local participation, and public celebration of Minnesota's waters.
