(b) "Aggregated meter" means a meter located on the premises of a customer's owned or leased property that is contiguous with property containing the customer's designated meter. (c) "Capacity" means the number of megawatts alternating current (AC) at the point of interconnection between a distributed generation facility and a utility's electric system. (d) "Cogeneration" means a combined process whereby electrical and useful thermal energy are produced simultaneously.
