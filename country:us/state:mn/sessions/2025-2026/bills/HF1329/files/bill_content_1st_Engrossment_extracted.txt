Following each apportionment of council districts, as provided under subdivision 3a, council members must be appointed from newly drawn districts as provided in subdivision 3a. Each council member, other than the chair, must reside in the council district represented. Each council district must be represented by one member of the council. The appointment to the council must be made by the first Monday in March of the year in which the term ends. new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE; APPLICATION; TRANSITION. new text end new text begin (a) This section is effective for appointments made on or after January 1, 2026, and applies in the counties of Anoka, Carver, Dakota, Hennepin, Ramsey, Scott, and Washington. new text end new text begin (b) Each member serving on the Metropolitan Council on the effective date of this section shall continue to serve until the member's successor is appointed and qualified. new text end Sec. 2.
