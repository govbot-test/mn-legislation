The commissioner of education must review proposals and award grants to programs that primarily serve historically underserved students. To the extent practicable, the commissioner must award grants equitably among the geographic areas of Minnesota, including rural, suburban, and urban communities. The submitted proposal for an emerging partnership grant must include evidence of: (1) readiness to pursue the activities required of an established partnership; and (2) adding geographic or demographic diversity to the education partnerships network. new text end new text begin (c) This is a onetime appropriation. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
