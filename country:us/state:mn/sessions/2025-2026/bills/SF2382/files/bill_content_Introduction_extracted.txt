BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 84.0286, is amended to read: 84.0286 CONSERVATION OFFICER PATROL VEHICLEdeleted text begin ; SECURITY BARRIER; EXEMPTIONdeleted text end . new text begin Subdivision 1. new text end new text begin Security barriers. new text end Marked conservation officer patrol vehicles are exempt from any law or rule requiring a security barrier in the vehicle. new text begin Subd. 2. new text end new text begin Dogs on patrol. new text end new text begin (a) A dog may accompany a conservation officer in a patrol vehicle if the conservation officer certifies on a form developed by the commissioner that: new text end new text begin (1) the dog is categorized by the American Kennel Club as a breed in the sporting group; new text end new text begin (2) the dog is current on required vaccines and is not sick; new text end new text begin (3) the dog has never seriously injured a person; and new text end new text begin (4) the conservation officer assumes personal responsibility and liability for any damage or injury to persons or property that the dog may cause. new text end new text begin (b) Neither the commissioner, the department, nor the state is liable for any damage or injuries caused to persons or property by a dog subject to a certification under paragraph (a). new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
