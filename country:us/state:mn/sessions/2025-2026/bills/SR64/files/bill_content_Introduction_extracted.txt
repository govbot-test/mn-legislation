No. 64, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on February 26, 2026 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2congratulating Donovan Notch for earning the rank of Eagle Scout. 1.3WHEREAS, the Eagle Scout rank is the highest rank earned in Scouting America; and 1.4WHEREAS, attaining the rank of Eagle Scout in Scouts BSA is an important achievement 1.5that is worthy of recognition; and 1.6WHEREAS, Donovan Notch is a member of Scouting America Troop 31 and has displayed 1.7the attributes of a responsible and serving member; and 1.8WHEREAS, Donovan has demonstrated mastery of all Scouting skills and has completed 1.9a community service project, which involved building covered dugouts for one of Avon's town 1.10baseball fields. He secured donations to fund the supplies, designed the dugouts, coordinated 1.11materials through a local lumber yard, and, over the course of several months, worked alongside 1.12both youth and adult volunteers to complete construction. The baseball field is regularly used by 1.13the children who play on Avon Lakers Little League teams. Before Donovan's project, the field 1.14had only aging wooden benches with no shade. The new covered dugouts provide improved seating, 1.15protection from the elements, and a lasting benefit to the community's young athletes. He also 1.16fulfilled all other requirements necessary to attain the rank of Eagle Scout; and 1.17WHEREAS, an Eagle Court of Honor will be held; and 1.18NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.19congratulates Donovan Notch for attaining the rank of Eagle Scout. 1.20BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare a 1.21copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the Chair of the 1.22Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Donovan Notch. Tom BotternSecretary of the Senate Erin P. MurphyChair, Senate Committee onRules and Administration Jeff R.
