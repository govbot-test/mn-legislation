WHEREAS, on February 24, 2022, the Russian Federation launched an unprovoked, full-scale invasion of Ukraine, in violation of international law, the United Nations Charter, and Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity; and WHEREAS, the people of Ukraine have demonstrated extraordinary courage, resilience, and unity in defending their homeland, democratic institutions, and fundamental freedoms; and WHEREAS, thousands of civilians have lost their lives, millions have been displaced, and critical infrastructure has been destroyed as a result of Russia's aggression; and WHEREAS, Minnesota is home to a vibrant and growing Ukrainian American community that contributes significantly to the cultural, civic, academic, and economic life of the state; and WHEREAS, Minnesotans have mobilized humanitarian assistance, welcomed displaced Ukrainians, and provided material and moral support to Ukraine's defense and reconstruction; and WHEREAS, the state of Minnesota affirms its long-standing commitment to democratic values, human rights, the rule of law, and the sovereignty of nations; NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the State of Minnesota that it reaffirms its strong support for the sovereignty, independence, and territorial integrity of Ukraine within its internationally recognized borders; recognizes the Russian Federation as the aggressor in the ongoing war against Ukraine; honors the resilience and sacrifice of the Ukrainian people and the Ukrainian Armed Forces; expresses solidarity with Minnesota's Ukrainian American community; and encourages continued humanitarian support for the people of Ukraine. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives is directed to prepare an enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by his signature and that of the Speaker, and transmit it to President of the United States, members of Minnesota's Congressional delegation, and the Ambassador of Ukraine to the United States.
