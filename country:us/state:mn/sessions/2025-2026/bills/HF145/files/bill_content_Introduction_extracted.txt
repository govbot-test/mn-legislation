BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 298.75, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 12. new text end new text begin Tax may be imposed; city of St. Paul Park and Grey Cloud Island Township. new text end new text begin (a) Notwithstanding subdivision 2, paragraph (d), in addition to any tax imposed by Washington County under this section, the city of St. Paul Park may impose the aggregate materials tax under this section. For the purposes of exercising the powers contained in this section, the city is deemed to be the "county." All provisions in this section apply to the city of St. Paul Park, except that all proceeds of the tax must be retained by the city and used for the purposes described in subdivision 7. new text end new text begin (b) Notwithstanding subdivision 2, paragraph (d), in addition to any tax imposed by Washington County under this section, Grey Cloud Island Township may impose the aggregate materials tax under this section. For the purposes of exercising the powers contained in this section, the township is deemed to be the "county." All provisions in this section apply to Grey Cloud Island Township, except that all proceeds of the tax must be retained by the township and used for the purposes described in subdivision 7. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin Paragraph (a) is effective the day after the governing body of the city of St. Paul Park and its chief clerical officer comply with Minnesota Statutes, section 645.021, subdivisions 2 and 3. Paragraph (b) is effective the day after the governing body of Grey Cloud Island Township and its chief clerical officer comply with Minnesota Statutes, section 645.021, subdivisions 2 and 3. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
