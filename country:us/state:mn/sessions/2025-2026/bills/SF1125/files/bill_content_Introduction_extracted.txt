BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 124D.65, subdivision 5, is amended to read: Subd. 5. School district EL revenue. (a) For fiscal year 2024 through fiscal year 2026, a district's English learner programs revenue equals the sum of: (1) the product of (i) $1,228, and (ii) the greater of 20 or the adjusted average daily membership of eligible English learners enrolled in the district during the current fiscal year; and (2) $436 times the English learner pupil units under section 126C.05, subdivision 17. (b) For fiscal year 2027 and later, a district's English learner programs revenue equals the sum of: (1) the product of (i) $1,775, and (ii) the greater of 20 or the adjusted average daily membership of eligible English learners enrolled in the district during the current fiscal year; and (2) $630 times the English learner pupil units under section 126C.05, subdivision 17. new text begin (c) The English learner cross subsidy factor equals 25 percent for fiscal year 2027. For fiscal year 2028 and later, the English learner cross subsidy factor equals the cross subsidy factor for the previous fiscal year times one plus the rate of change in inflation calculated under section 126C.10, subdivision 2, paragraph (c). The English learner cross subsidy factor must not exceed 80 percent in any fiscal year. new text end deleted text begin (c)deleted text end new text begin (d)new text end A pupil ceases to generate state English learner aid in the school year following the school year in which the pupil attains the state cutoff score on a commissioner-provided assessment that measures the pupil's emerging academic English. new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective for revenue for fiscal year 2028 and later. new text end Sec. 2. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 125A.76, subdivision 2e, is amended to read: Subd. 2e. Cross subsidy reduction aid. (a) A school district's annual cross subsidy reduction aid equals the school district's initial special education cross subsidy for the previous fiscal year times the cross subsidy aid factor for that fiscal year. (b) The cross subsidy aid factor equals deleted text begin 6.43 percent for fiscal year 2023;deleted text end 44 percent for fiscal years 2024, 2025, and 2026; deleted text begin anddeleted text end 50 percent for fiscal year 2027new text begin ; and the amount specified in paragraph (c) for fiscal year 2028new text end and later. new text begin (c) For fiscal year 2028 and later, the special education cross subsidy factor equals the cross subsidy factor for the previous fiscal year times one plus the rate of change in inflation calculated under section 126C.10, subdivision 2, paragraph (c). The special education cross subsidy factor must not exceed 80 percent in any fiscal year. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective for revenue for fiscal year 2028 and later. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
