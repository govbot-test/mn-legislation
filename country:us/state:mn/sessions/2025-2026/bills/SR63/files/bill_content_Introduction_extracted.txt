No. 63, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on February 23, 2026 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2recognizing Minnesota Senate employees for their dedicated service. 1.3WHEREAS, these dedicated employees of the Minnesota Senate have served the State of 1.4Minnesota through a career of professional commitment to state government and are deserving of 1.5special legislative recognition; and 1.6WHEREAS, the employees being recognized for their service during Legislative Sessions 1.7with the Minnesota Senate include: 1.8 5 LEGISLATIVE SESSIONS 1.9 Thury, Kevin 1.10WHEREAS, the members of the Minnesota Senate value and appreciate the depth of 1.11experience and institutional knowledge these individuals have developed and displayed throughout 1.12their careers; and 1.13WHEREAS, a ceremony recognizing these employees for their service will be held in the 1.14Senate Chamber on February 23, 2026; and 1.15NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.16commends these employees of the Minnesota Senate for their dedication and outstanding service 1.17to the State of Minnesota. 1.18BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.19enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.20Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to the Office of the 1.21Secretary of the Senate for public display.
