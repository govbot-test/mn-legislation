No. 54, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on February 19, 2026 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2congratulating Dr. Dave Tilstra for receiving the 2025 Copic/MMA Humanitarian 1.3Award. 1.4WHEREAS, the Copic/MMA Humanitarian Award is presented annually by the Minnesota 1.5Medical Association and Copic Insurance to a physician who demonstrates meaningful work in 1.6improving health care in their local community; and 1.7WHEREAS, Dr. Dave Tilstra is a clinical geneticist recognized for his dedication to promoting 1.8healthy living in his practice and beyond; and 1.9WHEREAS, over the more than 30 years of his career, Dr. Tilstra has worked with patients 1.10with rare, often misunderstood disorders, bringing compassion and curiosity to their treatment; and 1.11WHEREAS, Dr. Tilstra founded BLEND and Feeling Good MN, health care initiatives that 1.12serve central Minnesota by promoting nutrition, exercise, and thoughtful community design. His 1.13advocacy for active transportation infrastructure, including sidewalk, street crossing, and bike lane 1.14development, surpasses what is typically considered health care. Dr. Tilstra has also contributed 1.15to making healthy food more affordable and available in workplaces, schools, and throughout the 1.16St. Cloud area; and 1.17WHEREAS, his vision for a healthier Minnesota boldly engages the policies and environments 1.18that determine the everyday choices that shape our health; and 1.19WHEREAS, Dr. Tilstra has chosen the CentraCare Health Foundation's Ian Lo Unique 1.20Genetic Blueprint #1342 fund as the recipient of the $10,000 donation designated by the recipient 1.21of the Copic/MMA Humanitarian Award. The fund supports genetic testing for families who could 1.22not otherwise obtain it because of uncovered cost; and 1.23NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.24thanks Dr. Dave Tilstra for his exceptional contributions to his community. 2.1BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 2.2enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 2.3Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Dr. Dave Tilstra. Tom BotternSecretary of the Senate Erin P. MurphyChair, Senate Committee onRules and Administration Jeff R.
