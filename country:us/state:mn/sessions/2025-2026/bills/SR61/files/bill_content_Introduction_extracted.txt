No. 61, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on February 23, 2026 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2honoring Mayor Dave Bartholomay of Circle Pines, Minnesota, for his career 1.3in public service. 1.4WHEREAS, those who are called to represent their communities as elected officials perform 1.5a valuable public service worthy of recognition; and 1.6WHEREAS, Dave Bartholomay has served on the Circle Pines City Council for nearly a 1.7quarter century, including 20 as mayor of the city. Following four years as city councilor, Dave 1.8was first elected mayor in 2005; and 1.9WHEREAS, in addition to serving as mayor, Dave has also served on the Centennial Lakes 1.10Police Governing Board and the Centennial Utilities Commission. He also held positions as assistant 1.11commissioner of the State Planning Agency and executive director of Mediation Services for Anoka 1.12County, and leadership roles with the Minnesota Council on Local Results and Innovation, National 1.13League of Cities, and North Metro Mayors Association; and 1.14WHEREAS, earlier this year, Dave received the 2025 C.C. Ludwig Award, the League of 1.15Minnesota Cities' top honor for elected officials, which is presented for vision, statesmanship, and 1.16commitment to the public good. In 2012, he was named the Outstanding Elected Official in 1.17Minnesota by the Association of Minnesota Emergency Managers; and 1.18WHEREAS, during his tenure as mayor, Circle Pines was named the third best suburb in 1.19America in 2011, thanks in part to the 20-year strategic plan for maintaining streets and infrastructure 1.20that Dave helped implement. He also helped keep the Anoka County Library in the city and advocated 1.21for a major renovation; and 1.22WHEREAS, Dave announced this summer that he is not seeking reelection as mayor and 1.23will serve until his term ends at the end of the year; and 1.24NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.25thanks Mayor Dave Bartholomay for his 24 years of public service to the city of Circle Pines and 1.26congratulates him on his retirement 1.27BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.28enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.29Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Circle Pines Mayor 1.30Dave Bartholomay.
