BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin APPROPRIATION; MINNEAPOLIS-DULUTH INTERCITY BUS STUDY.new text end new text begin (a) $....... in fiscal year 2026 is appropriated from the general fund to the commissioner of transportation for a study on intercity bus service in the corridor between Minneapolis-St. Paul and Duluth. new text end new text begin (b) At a minimum, the study must: new text end new text begin (1) analyze existing demand and service levels for public transportation in the corridor; new text end new text begin (2) identify and evaluate alternatives for intercity bus service improvements, including estimation of: new text end new text begin (i) ridership; new text end new text begin (ii) capital and operating costs; and new text end new text begin (iii) revenue; new text end new text begin (3) consider barriers and risks; and new text end new text begin (4) make recommendations regarding feasibility of intercity bus service improvements. new text end new text begin (c) By February 1, 2026, the commissioner must submit a copy of the study to the chairs and ranking minority members of the legislative committees with jurisdiction over transportation finance and policy. new text end Sec. 2. new text begin APPROPRIATION CANCELLATION; NORTHERN LIGHTS EXPRESS.new text end new text begin The uncommitted balance of the appropriation in fiscal year 2024 under Laws 2023, chapter 68, article 1, section 2, subdivision 2, paragraph (d), for the Minneapolis-Duluth Northern Lights Express intercity passenger rail project, estimated to be $194,413,803, is canceled to the general fund on the effective date of this section. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective the day following final enactment. new text end Sec. 3. new text begin TRANSFER; HIGHWAY USER TAX DISTRIBUTION FUND.new text end new text begin (a) On the effective date of this section, the commissioner of transportation must calculate a transfer amount that equals the amount determined under section 2, less the amount appropriated under section 1. new text end new text begin (b) The amount calculated under paragraph (a) is transferred in fiscal year 2025 from the general fund to the highway user tax distribution fund. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective the day following final enactment. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
