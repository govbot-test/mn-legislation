BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin SPECIAL LIQUOR LICENSE; CITY OF HASTINGS.new text end new text begin Notwithstanding any law, ordinance, charter provision, or term of any lease between the city of Hastings and the state of Minnesota to the contrary, the city of Hastings may issue an on-sale intoxicating liquor license as provided under Minnesota Statutes, section 340A.404, subdivision 1, paragraph (d), for sales of wine and malt liquor on the premises of a ballpark occupying land that is leased by the city from the state of Minnesota. A license issued under this section authorizes the license holder to make the sales allowed under Minnesota Statutes, section 340A.404, subdivision 1, paragraph (d), at all times provided by law for the on-sale of intoxicating liquor in the city. The state of Minnesota must not enforce any term of lease number I-6390, or any similar lease agreement between the city and the state restricting the use of state-owned property by the city, that is inconsistent with the authority granted to the city and the city's on-sale licensee under this section. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective upon approval by the Hastings City Council and compliance with Minnesota Statutes, section 645.021. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
