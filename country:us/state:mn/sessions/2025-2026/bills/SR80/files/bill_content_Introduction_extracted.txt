No. 80, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on April 28, 2026 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2expressing the sense of the Senate recognizing Somali American contributions and 1.3declaring June Somali American Heritage Month. 1.4WHEREAS, Somali Americans began arriving in Minnesota in significant numbers in the 1.5early 1990's, seeking safety, opportunity, and the promise of a better future; and 1.6WHEREAS, Minnesota is now home to the largest Somali American population in the United 1.7States, making Somali Americans an essential and inseparable part of the state's social, cultural, 1.8and economic fabric; and 1.9WHEREAS, over the past three decades, Somali Americans have contributed profoundly to 1.10the growth and success of Minnesota through their work across critical sectors, including 1.11transportation, manufacturing, logistics, poultry and food production, health care, education, and 1.12small business development; and 1.13WHEREAS, Somali American entrepreneurs and small business owners have revitalized 1.14commercial corridors across Minnesota, including Lake Street and other key economic hubs, 1.15transforming disinvested areas into thriving centers of commerce, culture, and community life; and 1.16WHEREAS, Somali Americans serve Minnesota every day as doctors, nurses, personal care 1.17attendants, educators, engineers, drivers, laborers, manufacturers, and public servants, strengthening 1.18the state's workforce and advancing its prosperity; and 1.19WHEREAS, Somali American youth represent one of the youngest and fastest-growing 1.20populations in Minnesota, bringing innovation, leadership, and a promising future to the state; and 1.21WHEREAS, Somali Americans have increasingly stepped into public leadership, serving in 1.22local, state, and national roles, including city councils, school boards, and the Minnesota Legislature, 1.23helping to shape a more inclusive and representative democracy; and 2.1WHEREAS, despite these contributions, Somali Americans have faced escalating verbal 2.2attacks, harmful stereotypes, and targeted rhetoric at the federal level, rooted in historical 2.3anti-Blackness and xenophobia; and 2.4WHEREAS, such rhetoric has contributed to harmful policies and enforcement actions, 2.5including large-scale operations such as Operation Metro Surge, which have created fear, instability, 2.6and disproportionate targeting of Somali American communities; and 2.7WHEREAS, Minnesota rejects all forms of bigotry, discrimination, and collective blame, 2.8and affirms that attacks against Somali Americans are attacks against the shared values of dignity, 2.9equity, and justice; and 2.10WHEREAS, the state of Minnesota recognizes that the story of Somali Americans is one of 2.11resilience, contribution, and belonging and that their history, culture, and traditions enrich the 2.12broader identity of Minnesota; and 2.13WHEREAS, it is both appropriate and necessary to formally recognize and celebrate the 2.14lasting impact of Somali Americans, while affirming continued solidarity in the face of discrimination 2.15and injustice; NOW, THEREFORE, 2.16BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it recognizes and honors 2.17the significant contributions of Somali Americans to the economic vitality, cultural richness, and 2.18civic life of Minnesota; affirms its commitment to stand with Somali American communities against 2.19discrimination, hate, and policies that unfairly target or marginalize them; and encourages all 2.20Minnesotans to learn about and celebrate Somali American history, culture, and contributions, and 2.21to foster greater understanding and unity across communities. 2.22BE IF FURTHER RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it declares the 2.23month of June Somali American Heritage Month in the state of Minnesota, to be observed annually 2.24as a time of recognition, education, and celebration, and commits to uplifting the voices, leadership, 2.25and future of Somali Americans, acknowledging their indispensable role in shaping a stronger, 2.26more just Minnesota.
