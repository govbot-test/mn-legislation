BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 645.451, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 7. new text end new text begin Boy. new text end new text begin "Boy" means a minor human male. new text end Sec. 2. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 645.451, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 8. new text end new text begin Father. new text end new text begin "Father" means a male parent. new text end Sec. 3. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 645.451, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 9. new text end new text begin Female. new text end new text begin "Female" means, when used to refer to an individual, an individual who naturally has, had, will have, or would have, but for a developmental or genetic anomaly or historical accident, the reproductive system that at some point produces, transports, and utilizes eggs for fertilization. new text end Sec. 4. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 645.451, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 10. new text end new text begin Gender. new text end new text begin "Gender" has the meaning given to sex in subdivision 15, when used alone to refer to males, females, or the natural differences between males and females. Gender is not considered a synonym or shorthand expression for gender identity, experienced gender, gender expression, or gender role. This definition does not apply when the term is used in conjunction with other words or as an adjective to modify other words, or when context or explicit definition in existing state law, administrative rules, or guidelines of a governmental entity indicates otherwise. Gender is not synonymous with gender identity. new text end Sec. 5. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 645.451, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 11. new text end new text begin Girl. new text end new text begin "Girl" means a minor human female. new text end Sec. 6. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 645.451, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 12. new text end new text begin Male. new text end new text begin "Male" means, when used to refer to an individual, an individual who naturally has, had, will have, or would have, but for a developmental or genetic anomaly or historical accident, the reproductive system that at some point produces, transports, and utilizes sperm for fertilization. new text end Sec. 7. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 645.451, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 13. new text end new text begin Man. new text end new text begin "Man" means an adult human male, except when used as a generic reference to human beings. new text end Sec. 8. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 645.451, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 14. new text end new text begin Mother. new text end new text begin "Mother" means a female parent. new text end Sec. 9. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 645.451, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 15. new text end new text begin Sex. new text end new text begin "Sex" means, when referring to a characteristic of an individual, the individual's biological sex, either male or female. Sex is not synonymous with gender identity. new text end Sec. 10.
