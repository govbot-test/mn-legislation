BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 256B.051, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 9. new text end new text begin Authorization of services by waiver case managers. new text end new text begin (a) This subdivision applies to authorizations for housing stabilization services provided to an individual eligible for waiver services under chapter 256S or section 256B.092 or 256B.49. By signing a person-centered plan, an eligible provider agrees to provide the services described in the person-centered plan. After submission of a person-centered plan signed by an eligible provider to the individual's waiver case manager, the individual's waiver case manager shall determine if the individual is eligible for housing stabilization services and approve housing stabilization services subject to the following limits: new text end new text begin (1) up to 150 hours of housing transition services per transition, no more than half of which may be provided remotely; new text end new text begin (2) up to 150 hours of housing sustaining services annually, no more than half of which may be provided remotely; new text end new text begin (3) up to $3,000 for moving expenses per eligibility span; and new text end new text begin (4) housing consultation services once annually. new text end new text begin (b) The individual's waiver case manager must submit any request for exceptions to the service limits described in paragraph (a) to the commissioner of human services for approval. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective July 1, 2025. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
