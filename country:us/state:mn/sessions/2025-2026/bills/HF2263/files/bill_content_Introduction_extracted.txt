BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin APPROPRIATIONS; FIRST LANGUAGE RESOURCES.new text end new text begin Subdivision 1. new text end new text begin Department of Education. new text end new text begin The sums indicated in this section are appropriated from the general fund to the Department of Education in the fiscal years designated. new text end new text begin Subd. 2. new text end new text begin First language resources. new text end new text begin (a) For a grant to Independent School District No. 625, St. Paul Public Schools, for professional development and culturally relevant instructional materials at East African Magnet School: new text end new text begin $ new text end new text begin 170,000 new text end new text begin ..... new text end new text begin 2026 new text end new text begin $ new text end new text begin 144,000 new text end new text begin ..... new text end new text begin 2027 new text end new text begin (b) The professional development must include instruction for teachers on how to navigate free open source repositories containing literacy resources, selecting and translating culturally relevant materials, incorporating first language resources into literacy instruction, and adapting stories for diverse learners. The district must share the literacy and professional development resources developed as a result of receiving the grant through a Creative Commons license that requires attribution, noncommercial usage, and distribution of modified materials under the same license as the original. The resources must be available under an appropriate license and at no cost to schools throughout Minnesota. new text end new text begin (c) Any balance in fiscal year 2026 is available in fiscal year 2027. new text end new text begin (d) This is a onetime appropriation. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
