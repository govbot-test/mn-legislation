BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT PROPOSED.new text end new text begin An amendment to the Minnesota Constitution is proposed to the people. If the amendment is adopted, a section shall be added to article X, to read: new text end new text begin Sec. 9. new text end new text begin The Minnesota tax relief account is established in the state treasury. Money in the account shall be appropriated in each biennium by law for the purposes of returning excess tax collections to the property and income taxpayers of this state through refunds, which shall not be taxable under the laws of this state, or to offset the cost to the state general fund for tax reductions. No appropriations or offsets for tax reductions shall be made for any amount that exceeds a property or income taxpayer's liability for tax. The account shall be funded by depositing in the account the amount of projected revenues in the general fund that exceeds 105 percent of projected expenditures, as determined in the last biennial budget forecast for the general fund in the most recently completed calendar year. new text end Sec. 2. new text begin SUBMISSION TO VOTERS.new text end new text begin The proposed amendment must be submitted to the people at the 2026 general election.
