BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 609.78, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 2d. new text end new text begin Restitution. new text end new text begin (a) The court, while imposing a sentence on a person convicted under subdivision 2, clause (2), shall order the person to make full and complete restitution to: new text end new text begin (1) the public agency for the reasonable costs of the emergency response by that public agency; and new text end new text begin (2) a direct victim of the fictitious emergency. new text end new text begin (b) A restitution payment under paragraph (a) does not limit the liability for damages in a civil action or proceeding for an amount greater than the restitution payment. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
