BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. Minnesota Statutes 2024, section 116.07, is amended by adding a subdivision to read: new text begin Subd. 4o. new text end new text begin Permit application tracker. new text end new text begin The commissioner must establish, maintain, and continually improve a web-based application that allows applicants and members of the public to track the status of permit applications submitted to the agency. The information available through the tracker must include the facility name, permit program category, permit type, facility identification number, permit number, and the dates on which steps in the permitting process were completed. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective the day following final enactment. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
