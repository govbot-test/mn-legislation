No. 74, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on April 9, 2026 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2honoring Threads Dance Project on its 15th anniversary. 1.3WHEREAS, Threads Dance Project, founded in 2011 by artistic director Karen Long Charles, has 1.4become a vital cultural institution, known for its powerful contemporary dance works and commitment to 1.5artistic excellence; and 1.6WHEREAS, the company was created to illuminate the "threads" that connect us as human beings, 1.7using dance to foster empathy, understanding, and dialogue across communities; and 1.8WHEREAS, Threads Dance Project has, for 15 years, uplifted diverse voices and centered stories 1.9that reflect the richness, complexity, and beauty of Minnesota's communities; and 1.10WHEREAS, under the visionary leadership of Karen Long Charles, an acclaimed choreographer, 1.11educator, and advocate for equity in the arts, Threads has cultivated an environment where dancers, emerging 1.12artists, and community members can grow, create, and thrive; and 1.13WHEREAS, Threads Dance Project has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to equity, 1.14representation, and belonging, ensuring that its programs, performances, and partnerships reflect the full 1.15spectrum of Minnesota's cultural landscape; and 1.16WHEREAS, the company's educational initiatives, including youth training, community classes, 1.17residencies, and artist development programs, have expanded access to high-quality arts experiences for 1.18people of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities; and 1.19WHEREAS, Threads Dance Project has served as a gathering place for healing, creativity, and 1.20connection; strengthened the social and cultural fabric of Minnesota; and contributed to the state's reputation 1.21as a national leader in the arts; and 1.22WHEREAS, the 15th anniversary of Threads Dance Project marks a significant milestone in its 1.23ongoing mission to inspire, challenge, and unite audiences through dance; and 1.24WHEREAS, the state of Minnesota recognizes the profound impact of organizations that champion 1.25artistic expression, nurture emerging talent, and build inclusive spaces where all people can see themselves 1.26reflected and valued; and 1.27NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it celebrates 1.28Threads Dance Project on the occasion of its 15th anniversary and honors Karen Long Charles for her 1.29extraordinary leadership, creativity, and service to the people of Minnesota. 1.30BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Minnesota Senate commends Threads Dance Project for 1.31its enduring contributions to the arts, steadfast commitment to community, and role in shaping a more vibrant, 1.32equitable, and connected Minnesota. 1.33BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an enrolled 1.34copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the Chair of the Senate 1.35Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Threads Dance Project.
