(c) A person who violates subdivision 1new text begin , clause (1) or (2),new text end and gains access to personal data is guilty of a gross misdemeanor and may be sentenced under paragraph (a). (d) A person who is convicted of a second or subsequent misdemeanor violation of subdivision 1new text begin , clause (1) or (2),new text end within five years is guilty of a gross misdemeanor and may be sentenced under paragraph (a). (e) A person who violates subdivision 1new text begin , clause (2),new text end by accessing, or attempting to access, an electronic terminal through opening, or attempting to open, any panel or access door without authorization is guilty of a gross misdemeanor and may be sentenced under paragraph (a).
