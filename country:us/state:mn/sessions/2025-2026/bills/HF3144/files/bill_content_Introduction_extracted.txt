BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin APPROPRIATION; SMALL UNMANNED AIRCRAFT MEDICAL SUPPLIES DELIVERY.new text end new text begin (a) $1,000,000 in fiscal year 2026 and $1,000,000 in fiscal year 2027 are appropriated from the general fund to the commissioner of transportation for development and support related to delivery of medical supplies to rural areas of the state through the use of small unmanned aircraft systems, as defined under Minnesota Statutes, section 360.013, subdivision 57b. This is a onetime appropriation. new text end new text begin (b) Development and support under paragraph (a) includes but is not limited to: new text end new text begin (1) air navigation facility infrastructure improvements for operation of small unmanned aircraft systems beyond visual line of sight; new text end new text begin (2) assistance in applications for a beyond visual line of sight certificate of waiver under Code of Federal Regulations, title 14, section 107.200; new text end new text begin (3) testing on delivery of medical supplies; new text end new text begin (4) public engagement and education on use of small unmanned aircraft systems for delivery; and new text end new text begin (5) identification of guidance and best practices on the activities under clauses (2) to (4). new text end new text begin (c) The commissioner must consult with the commissioner of health and the Minnesota Board of Pharmacy on expenditures under this section. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
