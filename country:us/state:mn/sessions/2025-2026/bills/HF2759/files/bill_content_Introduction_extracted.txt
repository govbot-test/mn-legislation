BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin QUALIFICATION-BASED SELECTION FOR ENGINEERING SERVICES PILOT PROGRAM.new text end new text begin Subdivision 1. new text end new text begin Definitions. new text end new text begin (a) For purposes of this section, the following terms have the meanings given. new text end new text begin (b) "Best value" has the meaning given in Minnesota Statutes, section 16C.28, subdivision 1b. new text end new text begin (c) "Commissioner" means the commissioner of transportation. new text end new text begin (d) "Contractual threshold" means a project with consultant fees for engineering services in excess of $800,000 per project year. new text end new text begin (e) "Project" means an uncompleted road construction project where qualification-based selection services are utilized. new text end new text begin (f) "Qualification-based selection" has the meaning given in Minnesota Statutes, section 16C.087, subdivision 2. new text end new text begin Subd. 2. new text end new text begin Establishment. new text end new text begin From October 1, 2025, to March 1, 2028, the commissioner must use qualification-based selection methods to determine the selection of engineering and design professionals on five or more projects in each year meeting the contractual threshold. The commissioner may use qualification-based selection methods across different phases of an eligible project, including predesign, design, construction, and reconstruction. In selecting which five projects per year are subject to qualification-based selection, the commissioner must not use the same project or same phase of a project in a subsequent year. new text end new text begin Subd. 3. new text end new text begin Preliminary report. new text end new text begin By October 1, 2026, the commissioner must submit a preliminary report to the chairs and ranking minority members of the legislative committees with jurisdiction over transportation finance and policy. The report must contain information on the use of qualification-based selection methods by the Department of Transportation for certain road construction projects, including: new text end new text begin (1) a preliminary evaluation of the use of qualification-based selection methods; new text end new text begin (2) the development of criteria to determine eligible projects for which qualification-based selection is used; new text end new text begin (3) recommendations on whether legislative changes are necessary to increase the number of projects using qualification-based selection methods before expiration of the pilot program; and new text end new text begin (4) information detailing the use of qualification-based selection methods on selected projects, including the impact on: new text end new text begin (i) procurement timelines; new text end new text begin (ii) the quality of projects across all phases; new text end new text begin (iii) change orders; and new text end new text begin (iv) identified improvements to sustainability compared to best value engineering practices. new text end new text begin Subd. 4. new text end new text begin Final report. new text end new text begin By October 1, 2028, the commissioner must submit a final report to the chairs and ranking minority members of the legislative committees with jurisdiction over transportation finance and policy on the use of qualification-based selection methods for eligible road construction projects. The final report must include updated information on the preliminary report submitted under subdivision 3 and must include: new text end new text begin (1) the commissioner's recommended number of projects for which qualification-based selection methods are to be used; new text end new text begin (2) a cost benefit analysis on the use of qualification-based selection methods compared to best value engineering practices for eligible projects under the pilot program; and new text end new text begin (3) any legislative recommendations to implement a higher number of qualification-based selection methods or require the use of those methods for all road construction projects over a certain contractual threshold for engineering services. new text end new text begin Subd. 5. new text end new text begin Expiration. new text end new text begin This section expires January 15, 2029, or upon submission of the final report in subdivision 4, whichever is sooner. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective the day following final enactment. new text end Sec. 2. new text begin APPROPRIATION.new text end new text begin $....... in fiscal year 2026 is appropriated from the general fund to the commissioner of transportation for the use of qualification-based selection methods and preparation of the legislative reports by the Department of Transportation in section 1. This is a onetime appropriation and is available until June 30, 2029. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
