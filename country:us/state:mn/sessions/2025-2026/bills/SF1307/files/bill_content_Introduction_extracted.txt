BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin [461.185] TOBACCO RETAILERS; NICOTINE REPLACEMENT THERAPY PRODUCTS. new text end new text begin Subdivision 1. new text end new text begin Definitions. new text end new text begin (a) For purposes of this section, the following terms have the meanings given. new text end new text begin (b) "Cigar shop" means a retail establishment in which the only tobacco and tobacco-related devices available for sale are cigars and cigar accessories, including lighters, cigar cutters, humidors, and cigar carrying tubes. An establishment is a cigar shop for purposes of this section even if the establishment offers for retail sale other products that are not tobacco or tobacco-related devices. new text end new text begin (c) "Electronic delivery device" has the meaning given in section 609.685, subdivision 1. new text end new text begin (d) "Nicotine replacement therapy product" means a nicotine replacement therapy drug, device, or combination product that has been approved by the federal Food and Drug Administration for tobacco use cessation. new text end new text begin (e) "Tobacco" has the meaning given in section 609.685, subdivision 1. new text end new text begin (f) "Tobacco-related devices" has the meaning given in section 609.685, subdivision 1. new text end new text begin (g) "Tobacco retailer" means an individual or entity licensed under section 461.12 to engage in the retail sale of tobacco, tobacco-related devices, and electronic delivery devices. new text end new text begin Subd. 2. new text end new text begin Nicotine replacement therapy products offered for sale. new text end new text begin Except as provided in subdivision 5, a tobacco retailer that sells or offers for sale tobacco, tobacco-related devices, or electronic delivery devices must maintain a stock of and offer for retail sale at least one type of nicotine replacement therapy product. A tobacco retailer has the authority to determine: new text end new text begin (1) whether the tobacco retailer stocks and offers for sale more than one type of nicotine replacement therapy product; new text end new text begin (2) the number and type of nicotine replacement therapy products the tobacco retailer stocks and offers for sale; and new text end new text begin (3) the quantity of each nicotine replacement therapy product the tobacco retailer stocks and offers for sale. new text end new text begin Subd. 3. new text end new text begin Restocking. new text end new text begin A tobacco retailer that is subject to subdivision 2 and that sells out of the tobacco retailer's entire stock of nicotine replacement therapy products must: new text end new text begin (1) within five business days after the date the tobacco retailer sells the tobacco retailer's last nicotine replacement therapy product, place an order for a new stock of a nicotine replacement therapy product; and new text end new text begin (2) within 14 calendar days after the date the tobacco retailer sells the tobacco retailer's last nicotine replacement therapy product, again stock and offer for retail sale a nicotine replacement therapy product. new text end new text begin Subd. 4. new text end new text begin Display and notice. new text end new text begin (a) A tobacco retailer that is subject to subdivision 2 must: new text end new text begin (1) display the nicotine replacement therapy products that the tobacco retailer offers for retail sale behind a checkout counter where the public is not permitted; new text end new text begin (2) provide printed notice within the tobacco retailer's establishment that nicotine replacement therapy products are available for retail sale at that location; and new text end new text begin (3) display the official logo, telephone number, and internet address for the tobacco cessation program administered by or under contract with the commissioner of health under section 144.397. new text end new text begin (b) The commissioner of health may establish requirements governing: new text end new text begin (1) how the materials required under paragraph (a), clauses (2) and (3), must be displayed; and new text end new text begin (2) the size and other characteristics of the notice required under paragraph (a), clause (2). new text end new text begin Subd. 5. new text end new text begin Exception. new text end new text begin Subdivisions 2 to 4 do not apply to cigar shops. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
