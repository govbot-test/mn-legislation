BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin [160.086] LOCAL APPROVAL REQUIRED FOR CERTAIN EASEMENTS. new text end new text begin Subdivision 1. new text end new text begin Definitions. new text end new text begin (a) For purposes of this section, the following terms have the meanings given. new text end new text begin (b) "Easement" means the acquisition or designation of private or public land for the purpose of constructing, maintaining, or improving certain project elements related to a road construction project. new text end new text begin (c) "Local approval" means the approval by a statutory or home rule charter city, county, or town either by: new text end new text begin (1) a formal resolution adopted by the governing body of the local government unit; or new text end new text begin (2) a vote at the next general election and, if approved by a majority of those voting on the question, becoming effective following the final canvass of the election. new text end new text begin (d) "Roadway project elements" means the construction, maintenance, improvement, capacity expansion, reconstruction, resurfacing, or any other development of roadway infrastructure including shoulders, ditches, and culverts for the exclusive use of motor vehicle travel on the trunk highway system. new text end new text begin Subd. 2. new text end new text begin Local approval required. new text end new text begin Improvements to a road construction project that are not roadway project elements must receive local approval before funds from sections 85.53, 114D.50, and 129D.17, may be expended to acquire property by easement. new text end new text begin EFFECTIVE DATE. new text end new text begin This section is effective July 1, 2025, for easements entered into on or after that date. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
