WHEREAS, each year, dozens of babies in Minnesota are shaken and abused by a trusted caregiver; and WHEREAS, Shaken Baby Syndrome (SBS) or Abusive Head Trauma (AHT) kills 25 percent of its victims and leaves 70 percent of survivors with some form of neurological impairment. Zero percent of survivors will be able to live independently; and WHEREAS, SBS/AHT is 100 percent preventable; and WHEREAS, putting a baby down and letting them cry in a safe space is okay. Taking a break from a crying baby by walking out of the room or calling a friend or family member is the best way to prevent SBS/AHT; and WHEREAS, public education is critical for caregivers to understand that it is normal for young babies to cry and that caregivers should seek ways to calm themselves when getting frustrated; NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the State of Minnesota that it recognizes April 11, 2025, as Shaken Baby Prevention Day in the State of Minnesota. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives is directed to prepare an enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by his signature and that of the Speaker, and transmit it to Never Shake Minnesota.
