BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MINNESOTA: Section 1. new text begin [626.745] USE OF CHEMICAL IRRITANTS; DISCLOSURE REQUIRED. new text end new text begin (a) Notwithstanding any data classification under chapter 13, a law enforcement agency or local government unit must disclose the name, product number, and total number of all chemical irritants, smoke screens, and distraction devices deployed within a building by a peace officer in the course of: new text end new text begin (1) executing a search warrant; new text end new text begin (2) apprehending a criminal suspect; or new text end new text begin (3) attempting to apprehend a criminal suspect. new text end new text begin (b) Upon request, disclosure must be made to the building owner, any tenant in the building, any applicable insurer, and any person retained to provide cleaning or other remediation services related to the deployment of chemical irritants, smoke screens, or distraction devices. new text end new text begin (c) As used in this section, "building" has the meaning given in section 609.581, subdivision 2. new text end About the Legislature Historical Information Employment/Internships Visiting the Capitol Accessibility Frequently Asked Questions Contact Your Legislator Who Represents Me?
