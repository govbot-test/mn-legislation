No. 87, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on May 13, 2026 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2commemorating the lives and work of deceased Senators. 1.3 1.4 The Honorable Bruce D. Anderson, 2013-2025 The Honorable Don Samuelson, 1983-2002 1.5 1.6 The Honorable Kari Dziedzic, 2012-2024 The Honorable James Ulland, 1977-1984 1.7 1.8 The Honorable Alice M. Johnson, 2013-2016 The Honorable Ray Vandeveer, 2007-2012 1.9 1.10 The Honorable Bob Kierlin, 1999-2006 1.11WHEREAS, those in public office need an uncommon dedication to meet the demands upon 1.12their time, resources, and talents; and 1.13WHEREAS, in the history of the Minnesota Senate, there have been countless Senators who 1.14have left a heritage of noble deeds, thoughts, and acts; and 1.15WHEREAS, in their endeavors to legislate for the public good of this state, they strove to 1.16represent fairly the rights of the people; and 1.17WHEREAS, their spirits continually challenge, enlighten, and encourage those who remain 1.18to honestly and diligently exercise the work of the government for the public good; and 1.19WHEREAS, Senators of today take courage and inspiration from those noble servants of 1.20another time who believed it was better to serve than to be served; and 1.21NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.22recognizes the tremendous contributions of the following deceased senators: the Honorable Bruce 1.23D. Anderson, 2013-2025; the Honorable Kari Dziedzic, 2012-2024; the Honorable Alice M. Johnson, 1.242013-2016; the Honorable Bob Kierlin, 1999-2006; the Honorable Don Samuelson, 1983-2002; 1.25the Honorable James Ulland, 1977-1984; and the Honorable Ray Vandeveer, 2007-2012. Their 1.26dedication to the public good is a source of inspiration to, and is worthy of emulation by, their 1.27present-day colleagues. 1.28BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.29enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.30Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to appropriate relatives 1.31of those commemorated by this resolution.
